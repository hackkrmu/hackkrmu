About the Event

HACK KRMU 2.0, the second iteration of the HACK KRMU hackathon series, is a 3-day event that will equip students with real technical and collaboration skills to hack together a project. As technological innovation shapes our future, we believe that it is imperative to get more diversity into tech spaces, and we see hackathons as a tool to do so. Students currently in college and university, having any level of coding experience, are encouraged to join us in this epic 36-hour hackathon and discover their potential as problem-solvers and tech leaders.

We've got mentors, volunteers, and mixers to help you get what you need and branch out, and fun events like type racing when you're bushed and ready to unwind. We'll keep you well fed and watered all day long. It's time to go out on a limb and find your roots! Hosted by the School of Engineering & Technology (SOET) of K. R. Mangalam University, this is an event created for students, by students.
